Comment Finder Support

Help for the Comment Finder Chrome extension from incredible.tools.

Contact

Email contact@incredible.tools for product questions, privacy requests, or Chrome Web Store listing issues. Include your browser version and a short description of what you were doing when something went wrong.

Common issues

Comment Finder only works on YouTube watch pages, Shorts, `/channel/…` pages, and `@handle` pages. Legacy `/c/` custom URLs are not supported. Search stays locked until you accept the privacy policy in the extension.

If results fail to load, confirm you are signed into Chrome with the extension enabled, then try again on a different video. The proxy rate limit is about 20 requests per minute.

Privacy

See the privacy policy for what the extension processes and how to stop further use.
